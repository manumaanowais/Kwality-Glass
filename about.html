Discover Our Journey: Unveiling Excellence in Glass – The Kwality Glass Story
Welcome to Kwality Glass: Where Innovation Meets Excellence At Kwality Glass, we're not just in the business of glass – we're in the business of transforming spaces, elevating design, and inspiring creativity. With a rich history and a forward-looking approach, we have become a leading name in the world of architectural glass solutions.

THE COMPANY

Established in the year 1990 at Hyderabad, Telangana, India, we at "KWALITY GLASS" have strived very hard to provide our clients with the best possible services and utmost satisfaction in the field of aluminum fabrication such as structural glazing, spider-glazing, curtain-walls, wall cladding, doors, windows, etc within time-bound commitments.

We impart the best quality aluminum fabrication services and toughened glass installation services. Under the expert supervision, we have attained an immense position in the industry. Right from design to supply, fabrication & installation, the company believes in providing holistic services to its clients. We have executed major landmark projects in the state of Andhra & Telangana, thanks to the support from its prestigious clients.

Our specialized services in aluminum structural glazing include: ACP cladding, suspended glazing, systems of Domal / Wicona / Technal and Dorma - door hardware, frameless doors, frameless glass partitions, automatic revolving doors, custom-made toughened glass, shower enclosures, etc.

WHAT WE DO

Elevating Spaces with Glass Innovations: Our Services at Kwality Glass.

Spider Glazing

Spider Glazing is a part of the frameless glass system where they provide a flush external appearance with uninterrupted views.

Glass Canopy

A glass canopy provides an attractive and practical feature to a building.

ACP Cladding

ACP is a building cladding material commonly used in internal and external wall systems for its light weight, durability, and structural performance.

Glass Railings

All of our Glass Railing Systems receive durable powder-coated finishes.

Tinted Glass

Tinted Glass is widely used for residential and commercial purposes, along with automobiles, furniture, and decorative displays.

Digital Print Glass

Digital Glass Printing allows you to digitally print anything you imagine directly on the glass.
